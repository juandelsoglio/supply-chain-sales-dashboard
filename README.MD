# Supply Chain & Sales Dashboard

This project consists of the development of an interactive dashboard using **Google Looker Studio** to visualize and analyze complex data from supply chain operations and retail sales (2014-2017).

## Objective
To transform a raw dataset into an actionable Business Intelligence (BI) tool, enabling the identification of sales trends, profit by region, and logistics performance metrics.

## Technologies Used
- **Google Looker Studio:** For interactive dashboard creation.
- **Google Sheets:** For data source management and pre-processing.
- **Data Analysis:** KPI processing, regional segmentation, and historical trend analysis.

## Dashboard Visualization

## Methodology
1. **Data Cleaning:** Preparation and structuring of the original raw data.
2. **Modeling:** Creation of calculated fields in Looker Studio for key performance indicators (KPIs).
3. **Visualization:** Design of charts and metrics for sales, profit, and logistics efficiency analysis.

## Source Dataset
Data obtained from [Kaggle - Supply Chain and Sales Datasets](https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/ntbngoc/supply-chain-and-sales-datasets).
